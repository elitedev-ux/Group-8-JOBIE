About JOBIE

Students' Industrial Work Experience Scheme

What is JOBIE?

The Students' Industrial Work Experience Scheme (JOBIE) is a simple web-based application designed to connect students with industrial training opportunities. This platform allows students to search for available placements, apply for positions, and manage their applications — all in one place.

For Employers

Employers can post job openings and view applicants who have applied for their positions. The system uses LocalStorage to simulate a database, making it lightweight and easy to demonstrate without requiring a backend server.

Technical Details

This project was developed by Group 8 members as part of the CSC213 Assignment to demonstrate practical web development skills using plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
